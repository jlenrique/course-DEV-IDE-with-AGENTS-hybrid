You already run this loop many times a day—we’re simply swapping the patient for a broken workflow.

#### seg-04
<!-- REVOICE-REQUIRED: writer (Paige/Sophia) -> self-contained read-prose; Irene validates -->

## Depth-delta narrative

### Trait Overlap: Why Physicians Already Fit Innovation Work

### Clinical Workflow as Design Thinking
Objective: `u02` (section `sec-u02`)
Depth deferred off the slide: Stepwise prose walkthrough of the clinical method and design thinking parallels, including the exact pairings between history and empathize, diagnosis and define, treatment planning and prototype/test.
Convert a visual analogy into procedural confidence so learners can reuse the framework outside patient care.

### Choosing the Arena: Inside vs. Outside the Institution
Objective: `u05` (section `sec-u05`)
Depth deferred off the slide: Longer comparison of entrepreneurship and intrapreneurship, with emphasis on internal constraints, stakeholder alignment, and resource leverage inside health systems.
Help learners interpret innovation pathways strategically rather than assuming startup creation is the only meaningful route.

### From Zero-Value Idea to Actionable Opportunity
Objective: `u08` (section `sec-u08`)
Depth deferred off the slide: Detailed unpacking of feasibility, viability, and desirability as screening lenses for a proposed innovation inside a specific organization.
Move learners from casual brainstorming toward disciplined evaluation of execution readiness.

### First Principles Workbook: Deconstruct Before You Rebuild
Objective: `u11` (section `sec-u11`)
Depth deferred off the slide: A fuller explanation of reasoning by analogy versus first principles, plus a guided decomposition worksheet structure for breaking a workflow into fundamental truths.
Turn the conceptual method into a reusable redesign practice that can be applied to real institutional problems.

### Module Recap and Forward Link
Objective: `u14` (section `sec-u14`)

Consolidate memory traces across the lesson and prepare learners for the leadership-focused next module.
