# **Slide 2: The 70% Clinician Burnout Figure**

- **Visual Format:** Large single-number callout reading "70%" centered on the slide, with a one-line caption. No other statistics appear anywhere on this slide.
- **Learning Objective:** Recall and explain this lesson's single headline figure — that 70% of frontline clinicians report at least one symptom of professional burnout.
- **Narration (Speaker Notes):** "70% of frontline clinicians report at least one symptom of professional burnout. Say it plainly: 70%. This 70% figure is the single headline statistic of the entire lesson. Carry the 70% forward, because every downstream decision about staffing and workflow is framed by that 70% finding. When you explain this lesson to a colleague, lead with the 70%."
