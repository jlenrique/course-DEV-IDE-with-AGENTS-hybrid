# Chapter 3 Knowledge Check: The Case for Change (5 Questions)

**Q1: The Knowledge Explosion (Remembering)**
- **Prompt:** In 1950, medical knowledge doubled every 50 years. What is the currently projected doubling time?
- **Correct Answer:** 73 days.
**Q2: Technological Acceleration (Understanding)**
- **Prompt:** While 66% of physicians report using some form of AI in practice, what is the critical gap identified in the macro trends?
- **Correct Answer:** Formal training on how to oversee, evaluate, and safely implement these tools remains severely limited.
**Q3: Healthcare Consumerism (Applying)**
- **Prompt:** A hospital implements a "Digital Front Door" strategy. Which of the following initiatives best represents this concept?
- **Correct Answer:** An AI-driven, mobile-friendly triage system that allows patients to seamlessly schedule appointments and communicate with their care team before entering the physical clinic.
**Q4: The Leadership Gap (Analyzing)**
- **Prompt:** The data shows 67% of physicians want to pursue leadership roles, yet only 18% receive formal business training. What is the most significant consequence of this gap for Academic Health Centers?
- **Correct Answer:** They possess deep clinical expertise but lack the interdisciplinary management skills required to translate clinical ideas into scalable, system-wide improvements.
**Q5: The Trillion-Dollar Problem (Evaluating)**
- **Prompt:** Referring to Tyler Beauchamp’s article, which category of care failure requires the most urgent intrapreneurial intervention to reduce macro-level waste?
- **Correct Answer:** Administrative complexity and failures of care coordination.
