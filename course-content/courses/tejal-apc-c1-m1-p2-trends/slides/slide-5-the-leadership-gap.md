# **Slide 5: The Leadership Gap**

- **Visual Format:** Bar chart highlighting the gap between "Interest in Leadership" and "Formal Training."
- **Narration (Speaker Notes):** "This brings us to the ultimate gap, and the reason you are here. The data shows that 67% of practicing physicians want to pursue leadership roles to have a voice in organizational decisions. Yet, only 18% receive any executive or business training, and very few organizations offer formal leadership development. The COVID-19 pandemic clearly demonstrated that management and systems-thinking skills, often viewed as 'soft,' are actually critical to health and well-being. Academic Health Centers face this acutely—they have the clinical expertise but often lack the cultural incentives and interdisciplinary integration to translate ideas into scalable improvements. We need individuals who can bridge clinical practice with business and technology strategy."

**References:** Jackson Physician Search (2023); Rotenstein LS et al., Academic Medicine (2021).
