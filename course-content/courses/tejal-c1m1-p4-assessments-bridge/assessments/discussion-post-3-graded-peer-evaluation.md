# Discussion Post 3: Graded Peer Evaluation - The M&M of Innovation

**Format:** Graded, mandatory asynchronous discussion board in Canvas.

## Context Text

"This is the portfolio exercise for Module 1. We are going to apply the rigor of a clinical Morbidity and Mortality (M&M) conference to operational failures. You will propose a reframed problem, and your peers will act as your diagnostic sounding board."

## Prompt

### 1. The Initial Presentation

Select one recurring operational, clinical, or administrative failure from your own experience so far.

- Provide a 1-sentence "Chief Complaint" describing the superficial problem.
- Perform a brief "5 Whys" root-cause analysis.
- Present your "Reframed Opportunity": how you have shifted this from a complaint to an actionable target.

### 2. The Peer Differential

You must formally review at least two peers' presentations. In your review:

- Identify one blind spot in their root-cause analysis.
- Provide a constructive differential diagnosis for why their proposed opportunity might face organizational resistance.
- Reference Sandra Lam's Intrapreneurship Formula or the psychological-safety concepts discussed earlier in Module 1.
