# **Slide 6: The Lineage of Physician Innovators**

- **Visual Format:** A split-screen or four-quadrant profile slide highlighting diverse innovation types (Process, Device, System, Organizational).
- **Narration (Speaker Notes):** "When we talk about designing the system, we aren't speaking abstractly. Physicians have always been at the vanguard of innovation. Consider Dr. Thomas Fogarty, who invented the balloon embolectomy catheter, fundamentally changing vascular surgery (Device Innovation). Look at Dr. Peter Pronovost, whose creation of the central line checklist saved thousands of lives and revolutionized patient safety protocols globally (Process Innovation). Consider Dr. Patricia Bath, who invented the Laserphaco Probe for cataract surgery (Technological Innovation). Or look at organizational leaders who transitioned systems from reactive 'sick care' to proactive 'health assurance' models seen over the last decade (Organizational Innovation). You are stepping into this exact lineage."
