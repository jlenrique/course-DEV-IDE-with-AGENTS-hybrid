## Part 4: Assessments & The Bridge to Module 2

### Discussion Post 3: Graded Peer Evaluation (The "M&M" of Innovation)

- **Format:** Graded, mandatory asynchronous discussion board in Canvas.
- **Context Text:** "This is the portfolio exercise for Module 1. We are going to apply the rigor of a clinical Morbidity and Mortality (M&M) conference to operational failures. You will propose a reframed problem, and your peers will act as your diagnostic sounding board."
- **The Prompt:**
  1. **The Initial Presentation:** Select one recurring operational, clinical, or administrative failure from your own experience so far.
     - Provide a 1-sentence "Chief Complaint" (the superficial problem).
     - Perform a brief "5 Whys" root-cause analysis.
     - Present your "Reframed Opportunity" (How you have shifted this from a complaint to an actionable target).
  2. **The Peer "Differential" (Reply Requirement):** You must formally review at least two peers' presentations. In your review, you must identify one "Blind Spot" in their root-cause analysis. Provide a constructive "Differential Diagnosis" for why their proposed opportunity might face organizational resistance (referencing Sandra Lam's Intrapreneurship Formula or the concepts of psychological safety we discussed earlier).
