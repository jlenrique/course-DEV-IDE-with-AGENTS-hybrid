# Compositor Expertise

Compositor is the Class-D2 deterministic assembly pipeline exemplar for Slab 7b Story 7b.11.

It consumes the scaffold-v0.2-D2-pipeline contract at `docs/dev-guide/scaffolds/scaffold-v0.2-D2-pipeline/` and stores operational metadata at `_bmad/memory/bmad-agent-compositor/`.

The body is deterministic: no LLM call, no third-party provider client, byte-identical asset localization, and field-masked hashing for `DESCRIPT-ASSEMBLY-GUIDE.md`.
