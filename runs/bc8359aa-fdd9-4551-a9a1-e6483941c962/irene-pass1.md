# Irene Pass-1 Lesson Plan

A concise assessment-bridge lesson that closes Module 1 by synthesizing its core pillars, uses the knowledge check as a reflection surface, and reframes opportunity identification as insufficient without leadership identity. The sequence moves from completion acknowledgment, to what learners now know, to why execution inside a matrixed health system demands political savvy and communication, and finally to a source-grounded preview of Module 2's leadership-identity focus.
